🏥 The AI Procurement Committee

A scenario-based vendor evaluation activity · 6 domains · 25 points each · 150 points possible

Your Role

You are the DNP member of your institution's new AI Governance Committee. The committee is reviewing six AI tool proposals for adoption across different operational and clinical domains. For each tool, a vendor has made a presentation. Your job is to rate the vendor's response on the relevant scorecard domain (1–5) and provide a brief rationale. Then you'll see the expert evaluation and the governance implication.

Scoring Scale

5 = Fully meets standard with documented evidence  ·  4 = Meets standard, minor gaps  ·  3 = Partial evidence, significant gaps  ·  2 = Minimal evidence, major concerns  ·  1 = Does not meet standard
